about me

Hello! I'm Kathleen, an electrical and computer engineering MEng student graduating in May 2023 from Cornell University (where I also did my undergraduate degree in ECE). I enjoy creative, hands-on hardware projects, with experience in hardware/electronics prototyping, PCB design, systems design, and rapid prototyping. Additionally, I have experience and interests in embedded systems and web development.

In terms of work experience, I previously did a semester-long co-op at Apple in Spring 2021 as a iPhone Hardware Systems Engineer where I did PCB design and software development. I have also been a Space Lasers intern at SpaceX where I did hardware for the Starlink project. Additonally, my first internship was at a small, fast-paced engineering firm called Second Order Effects where I did software design, board bringup, and CAD design.

I am also currently a researcher in the Collective Embodied Intelligence Lab at Cornell University where I'm working on bio-inspired robots capable of construction and collaborative behaviors. I was previously an electrical team lead (and the driver) as an undergrad for Cornell Electric Vehicles, a high-efficiency electric vehicle project team.

In my free time, I love dancing (especially aerials), playing my guitar, learning random stuff, and hanging out with my friends.

Skilled in: Altium, Python, Cadence/Allegro, C, C++, Matlab, Fusion 360, Soldering, 3D printing, Linux/Unix

Experience with: Solidworks, Siemens NX, Verilog, LTspice, HTML, CSS, JS, Java, Lua, Final Cut Pro
