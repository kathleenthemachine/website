about me

Kathleen Wang

Hello! I'm Kathleen, a junior studying electrical and computer engineering at Cornell University. I enjoy interesting and challenging hands-on hardware projects, with experience and interests in PCB design and hardware system development. I'm also hoping to get involved with rapid prototyping and product design in the future.

I'm currently the electrical co-lead and driver for Cornell Electric Vehicles (CEV), where we design and build a high efficiency electric vehicle to compete in the Shell Eco-Marathon every year. Additionally, I have been an electrical engineering intern at SpaceX this past summer, working with the Space Lasers team on the Starlink project. I am also currently an undergraduate researcher in the Organic Robotics Lab, designing high voltage circuitry for controlling a capacitive array of HASEL actuators.

I will be doing a co-op during the upcoming spring semester (Spring 2021) at Apple as a iPhone Hardware Systems Integration Engineering Intern.

Skills: Altium, Python, C/C++, Verilog, Java, Matlab, Siemens NX, Assembly, MATLAB, Arduino, LTSpice, HTML/CSS
